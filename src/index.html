About me

Welcome to my digital space! I'm Atharv Shinde, a passionate second-year BSc Computer Science student and an enthusiastic online tutor. My journey in education has been a fulfilling one, with the privilege of guiding over 300 students through various channels such as online courses, lectures, webinars, and my own YouTube channel.

My expertise extends across a spectrum of programming languages, including Python, Java, and comprehensive skills in both Front-end and Back-end Web Development. As a student myself, I understand the importance of a well-rounded education, and I strive to provide just that to my students.

Beyond the virtual classroom, I'm a proactive creator, developing projects in Java and Python that reflect my commitment to practical, hands-on learning. You can explore these projects and more on my GitHub profile, offering a glimpse into my coding journey. Join me on this exciting adventure in the world of technology and education, where learning meets innovation.
